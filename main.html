About Our Firm

Chetluru Srinivas and Associates began its journey in 1991 and has been successfully operating in the legal field for over 30 years. The firm is a team of dedicated and intellectual professionals with expertise across various areas of law in India. Notably, the firm specializes in corporate law, land laws, civil disputes, criminal cases, including those under the Negotiable Instruments Act, Consumer Protection Act, Provident Fund Act, and matrimonial disputes.
