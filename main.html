About Our Firm

Chetluru Srinivas and Associates began its journey in 1991 and has been successfully operating in the legal field for over 35 years. The firm is a team of dedicated and intellectual professionals with expertise across various areas of law in India. Notably, the firm specializes in corporate law, land laws, civil disputes, criminal cases, including those under the Negotiable Instruments Act, Consumer Protection Act, Provident Fund Act, and matrimonial disputes.

The team efficiently handles cases under both the original and appellate jurisdictions of the High Court, proudly maintaining a success rate of 90% in the cases it files.

A hallmark of the firm is its commitment to transparency. The team provides clients with a clear assessment of their case's strengths and weaknesses based on the facts, and only takes up genuine matters. The association places strong emphasis on out-of-court settlements, advising clients on the time and costs involved in litigation.

The firm aims to provide comprehensive legal solutions under one roof. Chetluru Srinivas and Associates also offers expertise in patent registration and drafting. One of the team members is a registered patent and trademark agent, capable of facilitating the swift and secure registration of valuable patents. The firm strictly adheres to a policy of confidentiality, ensuring that client's inventions are kept secure and private.
