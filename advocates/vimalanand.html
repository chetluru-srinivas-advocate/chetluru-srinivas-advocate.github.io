Biography

He is an experienced Advocate and Registered Patent Agent (IN/PA 6235) with a strong foundation in both law and engineering. Being a Mechanical Engineering graduate with an LL.B. from Osmania University, he brings a unique interdisciplinary perspective to his practice. With over 12 years of Industrial Experience, he is registered to practice before Indian Courts as well as the Patent, Trademark, and Design Office.

As an advocate, he assists clients with a variety of legal issues, including family law, civil litigations, and business transactions. His approach is rooted in providing clear, actionable, and client-focused solutions, empowering individuals and businesses to make informed decisions with confidence.

As a Patent Agent and legal professional with a strong background in intellectual property law, patent drafting, and legal advisory services, he is skilled in conducting patentability searches, drafting and assisting in filing patent applications, interpreting complex legal frameworks, and providing counsel to clients on intellectual property protection.

He also supports clients in filing and processing Trademark Registration Applications.

Whether clients are seeking legal guidance, intellectual property protection, or strategic advice, he is committed to delivering exceptional service tailored to their needs.
