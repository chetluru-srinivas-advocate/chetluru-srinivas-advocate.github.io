Chetluru Srinivas

Biography & Legal Expertise

Enrolled as an advocate in 1991, after receiving training under retired senior district judges, the founder established an independent legal practice under the banner of Chetluru Srinivas & Associates, Advocates. With 35 years of experience, he has built a strong reputation for integrity and legal acumen. Supported by a professional team of young, energetic, and dedicated advocates, the firm has earned recognition in both civil and criminal courts across the Twin Cities of Hyderabad and Secunderabad, as well as in the Ranga Reddy District Courts.

The practice extends to both original and appellate jurisdictions of the High Court of Telangana at Hyderabad and the High Court of Andhra Pradesh at Amaravati.

The counsel is known for prompt availability and respects client appointments, ensuring no time is wasted.

Several matters handled by the firm have been reported in leading legal journals.
