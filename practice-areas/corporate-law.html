Corporate Law

Comprehensive legal solutions for businesses and corporations

Overview

At Chetluru Srinivas & Associates, our Corporate Law practice offers comprehensive legal services to businesses of all sizes, from startups to established corporations. With over 35 years of experience, our team has developed specialized expertise in navigating the complex legal landscape governing corporate entities in India.

We understand the unique challenges faced by businesses in today's competitive environment and provide tailored legal solutions that align with our clients' business objectives. Our attorneys have successfully represented clients before various forums, including the National Company Law Tribunal (NCLT), High Courts, and the Supreme Court of India.

Our Corporate Law Services

Our corporate law practice encompasses a wide range of services, including:

  • Companies Act Compliance: Advising on compliance with the Companies Act, 2013, including corporate governance, statutory filings, and regulatory requirements
  • NCLT Proceedings: Representation in matters before the National Company Law Tribunal, including corporate restructuring, mergers and acquisitions, and insolvency proceedings
  • Corporate Restructuring: Advising on and implementing corporate restructuring, including mergers, demergers, amalgamations, and acquisitions
  • Income Tax Cases: Representing clients in income tax disputes, appeals, and litigation
  • Provident Fund Act Matters: Advising on compliance with the Employees' Provident Fund and Miscellaneous Provisions Act and representing clients in related disputes
  • Corporate Governance: Developing and implementing corporate governance frameworks and policies
  • Commercial Contracts: Drafting, reviewing, and negotiating various commercial agreements and contracts
  • Due Diligence: Conducting comprehensive legal due diligence for corporate transactions
