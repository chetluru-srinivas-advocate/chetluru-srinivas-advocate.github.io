Arbitration

Efficient and effective alternative dispute resolution

Overview

Arbitration offers a private, efficient, and often more cost-effective alternative to traditional litigation. At Chetluru Srinivas & Associates, our arbitration practice provides comprehensive representation in domestic and international arbitration proceedings across various industries and dispute types.

Our experienced arbitration attorneys have successfully represented clients before major arbitral institutions and in ad hoc proceedings. We understand the nuances of arbitration law and procedure, allowing us to develop effective strategies tailored to each client's specific needs and goals.

Our Arbitration Services

Our arbitration practice encompasses a wide range of services, including:

  • Commercial Arbitration: Resolving business disputes, contract conflicts, and commercial disagreements
  • International Arbitration: Representing clients in cross-border disputes under various international arbitration rules
  • Construction Arbitration: Handling disputes related to construction projects, delays, defects, and payment issues
  • Investment Arbitration: Representing investors and states in investment treaty disputes
  • Employment Arbitration: Resolving workplace disputes and employment contract issues
  • Consumer Arbitration: Handling disputes between businesses and consumers
  • Arbitration Agreement Drafting: Creating effective arbitration clauses for contracts
  • Arbitration Award Enforcement: Assisting with recognition and enforcement of arbitral awards

Our Approach

Our approach to arbitration is characterized by:

  • Thorough case assessment and strategic planning from the outset
  • Careful selection of arbitrators when permitted by the arbitration agreement
  • Efficient case management to control costs and time
  • Effective presentation of evidence and legal arguments
  • Creative problem-solving to achieve favorable outcomes
  • Consideration of settlement opportunities throughout the process

We recognize that arbitration is designed to be more flexible than court litigation, and we leverage this flexibility to our clients' advantage. Our attorneys are skilled at adapting their approach to the specific rules, procedures, and dynamics of each arbitration proceeding.
