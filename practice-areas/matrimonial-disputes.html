Matrimonial Disputes

Compassionate legal guidance for family matters

Overview

Family law matters are often emotionally challenging and require a delicate balance of legal expertise and compassionate guidance. At Chetluru Srinivas & Associates, our family law practice is dedicated to helping clients navigate these sensitive issues with dignity and respect.

Our experienced family law attorneys understand the profound impact these matters have on your life and the lives of your loved ones. We strive to provide personalized legal solutions that protect your interests while minimizing conflict and emotional stress.

Our Family Law Services

Our comprehensive family law practice covers a wide range of matters, including:

  • Divorce and Separation: Contested and uncontested divorces, legal separation, and annulment
  • Child Custody and Visitation: Legal and physical custody arrangements, visitation rights, and parenting plans
  • Child Support: Establishment, modification, and enforcement of child support orders
  • Spousal Support/Alimony: Temporary and permanent support arrangements
  • Property Division: Equitable distribution of marital assets and liabilities
  • Adoption: Domestic and international adoptions, stepparent adoptions
  • Guardianship: Legal guardianship for minors and incapacitated adults
  • Domestic Violence: Protective orders and related family matters
  • Prenuptial and Postnuptial Agreements: Drafting and reviewing marital agreements

Our Approach

We recognize that each family is unique, and there is no one-size-fits-all approach to family law matters. Our approach is characterized by:

  • Personalized attention to your specific circumstances and goals
  • Clear communication about legal options and potential outcomes
  • Emphasis on negotiated settlements when possible to minimize conflict
  • Strong advocacy in court when litigation is necessary
  • Consideration of the emotional and financial impact of legal decisions
  • Special attention to the best interests of children involved

We strive to resolve family law matters through negotiation and alternative dispute resolution methods whenever possible. However, when litigation is necessary, our attorneys are prepared to provide strong and effective representation in court.
