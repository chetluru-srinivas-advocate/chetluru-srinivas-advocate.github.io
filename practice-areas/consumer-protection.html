Consumer Protection Cases

Advocating for consumer rights and fair business practices

Overview

At Chetluru Srinivas & Associates, our Consumer Protection practice is dedicated to protecting the rights of consumers against unfair trade practices, defective products, and inadequate services. With the implementation of the Consumer Protection Act, 2019, which replaced the earlier 1986 Act, we have adapted our approach to leverage the enhanced provisions for consumer rights and remedies.

Our team has extensive experience representing clients before Consumer Disputes Redressal Commissions at District, State, and National levels. We understand the nuances of consumer law in India and work diligently to secure fair compensation and justice for consumers who have been wronged by businesses and service providers.

Our Consumer Protection Services

Our consumer protection practice encompasses a wide range of services, including:

  • Consumer Complaints: Filing and representing consumer complaints before appropriate Consumer Disputes Redressal Commissions
  • Product Liability Cases: Handling cases involving defective products that have caused harm or loss to consumers
  • Service Deficiency Claims: Representing consumers in cases of inadequate or deficient services from service providers
  • Unfair Trade Practices: Challenging misleading advertisements, false representations, and other unfair business practices
  • E-commerce Disputes: Addressing issues related to online shopping, including delivery problems, product quality, and refund disputes
  • Medical Negligence: Representing consumers in cases of medical negligence and healthcare service deficiencies
  • Banking and Insurance Disputes: Handling consumer complaints against banks, insurance companies, and other financial service providers
  • Real Estate Consumer Issues: Addressing builder-buyer disputes, delays in possession, and quality issues in real estate transactions

Our Approach

Our approach to consumer protection cases is characterized by:

  • Thorough assessment of consumer grievances and applicable legal provisions
  • Detailed documentation and evidence gathering to support consumer claims
  • Strategic representation before Consumer Commissions at all levels
  • Emphasis on securing appropriate compensation and remedies
  • Negotiating settlements when beneficial to the consumer
  • Pursuing appeals when necessary to protect consumer interests
  • Educating clients about their rights under consumer protection laws

We understand that consumers often face significant power imbalances when dealing with businesses and service providers. Our goal is to level the playing field and ensure that consumers receive fair treatment and appropriate remedies when their rights have been violated. We take pride in our role as advocates for consumer justice in an increasingly complex marketplace.
