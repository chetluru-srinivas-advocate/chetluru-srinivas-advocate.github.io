Banking and Finance

Expert legal solutions for banking, finance, and debt recovery matters

Overview

At Chetluru Srinivas & Associates, our Banking and Finance practice offers comprehensive legal services to banks, financial institutions, lenders, borrowers, and other stakeholders in the financial sector. With over 35 years of experience, our team has developed specialized expertise in navigating the complex regulatory landscape governing banking and financial services in India.

We understand the unique challenges faced by clients in the banking sector and provide tailored legal solutions that balance regulatory compliance with business objectives. Our attorneys have successfully represented clients in various forums, including Debt Recovery Tribunals, High Courts, and the Supreme Court of India.

Our Banking and Finance Services

Our banking and finance practice encompasses a wide range of services, including:

  • Debt Recovery Tribunal (DRT) Cases: Representation in proceedings before DRTs and Debt Recovery Appellate Tribunals (DRATs) for recovery of debts due to banks and financial institutions
  • SARFAESI Act Matters: Advising and representing clients in matters related to securitization, asset reconstruction, and enforcement of security interests under the SARFAESI Act
  • Banking Litigation: Handling disputes related to loans, guarantees, letters of credit, and other banking transactions
  • Insolvency and Bankruptcy: Representing creditors, debtors, and other stakeholders in proceedings under the Insolvency and Bankruptcy Code
  • Loan Documentation: Drafting and reviewing loan agreements, security documents, and other financing agreements
  • Regulatory Compliance: Advising on compliance with RBI regulations, Banking Regulation Act, and other financial sector laws
  • Restructuring and Recovery: Assisting with loan restructuring, one-time settlements, and recovery strategies
  • Banking Fraud Cases: Representing clients in matters involving banking fraud and financial irregularities

Our Approach

Our approach to banking and finance matters is characterized by:

  • Thorough understanding of banking laws, regulations, and industry practices
  • Strategic planning to achieve optimal outcomes in recovery proceedings
  • Efficient case management to minimize delays in debt recovery
  • Creative solutions for complex financial disputes
  • Balancing aggressive advocacy with settlement opportunities
  • Regular updates and clear communication with clients

We recognize the importance of timely resolution in banking matters and strive to provide efficient and cost-effective legal services. Our team works closely with financial experts, accountants, and other professionals to develop comprehensive strategies for each case.
