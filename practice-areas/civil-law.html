Civil Law

Expert legal representation in all civil litigation matters

Overview

Civil law encompasses a wide range of legal issues between individuals, businesses, and organizations where compensation or specific performance is sought rather than criminal sanctions. At Chetluru Srinivas & Associates, our civil litigation team has decades of combined experience representing clients in complex civil matters.

Our approach combines thorough legal analysis, strategic planning, and aggressive advocacy to achieve the best possible outcomes for our clients. We understand that civil disputes can be stressful and disruptive, which is why we focus on efficient and effective resolution strategies.

Our Civil Law Services

Our civil litigation practice covers a comprehensive range of civil matters, including but not limited to:

  • Contract Disputes: Breach of contract claims, contract interpretation, and enforcement
  • Property Disputes: Boundary disputes, easement issues, and property damage claims
  • Tort Claims: Personal injury, professional negligence, and defamation
  • Commercial Litigation: Business disputes, partnership conflicts, and shareholder issues
  • Consumer Protection: Consumer rights violations and unfair business practices
  • Debt Recovery: Collection of outstanding debts and enforcement of judgments

Our Approach

We believe in a client-centered approach to civil litigation. This means:

  • Thorough case evaluation and risk assessment at the outset
  • Clear communication about legal options and potential outcomes
  • Strategic planning tailored to each client's specific goals
  • Aggressive representation in court when necessary
  • Exploration of alternative dispute resolution methods when appropriate

Our attorneys are skilled negotiators who can often resolve disputes without the need for protracted litigation, saving our clients time and money. However, when litigation is necessary, we are prepared to advocate vigorously in court to protect our clients' interests.
