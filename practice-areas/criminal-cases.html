Criminal Cases

Expert legal defense and representation in criminal matters

Overview

At Chetluru Srinivas & Associates, we provide comprehensive legal representation for all types of criminal cases. Our experienced criminal defense attorneys have successfully handled numerous high-profile cases and are committed to protecting your rights throughout the legal process.

With over 35 years of experience in criminal law, our team has developed a deep understanding of the Indian criminal justice system. We combine thorough case preparation, strategic defense planning, and aggressive courtroom advocacy to achieve the best possible outcomes for our clients.

Our Criminal Law Services

Our criminal law practice encompasses a wide range of services, including:

  • CBI and ACB Cases: Expert representation in cases investigated by the Central Bureau of Investigation and Anti-Corruption Bureau
  • PMLA Act Cases: Defense against charges under the Prevention of Money Laundering Act with specialized knowledge of financial crime regulations
  • Criminal Appeals: Skilled advocacy at appellate levels to challenge convictions or sentences
  • White Collar Crimes: Defense against fraud, embezzlement, and other financial crimes
  • Bail Applications: Prompt assistance with regular and anticipatory bail petitions
  • Criminal Trials: Thorough preparation and strategic representation during trial proceedings
  • Cheque Bounce Cases: Defense against charges under Section 138 of the Negotiable Instruments Act
  • Economic Offenses: Representation in cases involving alleged economic crimes

Our Approach

Our approach to criminal defense is characterized by:

  • Thorough case assessment and strategic planning from the outset
  • Detailed investigation and evidence gathering
  • Challenging procedural irregularities and constitutional violations
  • Effective cross-examination of prosecution witnesses
  • Presenting compelling defense evidence and arguments
  • Negotiating favorable plea bargains when appropriate
  • Pursuing all available legal remedies and appeals

We understand that facing criminal charges can be one of the most stressful experiences in a person's life. Our attorneys provide not only legal expertise but also emotional support and clear communication throughout the process. We ensure that clients fully understand their legal options and the potential consequences of each decision.
